How Much Does a Plumber Cost in Guelph?

Published 2026-06-01 — Guelph Plumber

Plumbing pricing in Guelph follows a consistent structure, but the total cost of a job varies significantly based on the work, parts, and whether it's emergency or scheduled service.

Hourly Rates

Licensed plumbers in Guelph typically charge $120–$180 per hour. The range reflects experience level, company overhead, and whether the company provides a warranty on labour. Most reputable companies operate at the higher end of this range.

Service Call Fee

A service call fee of $80–$120 covers the technician's travel and initial assessment. This fee applies whether you proceed with the repair or not, though some companies waive it if you book the repair on the spot. Always ask upfront.

Common Repair Costs in Guelph

  • Leaking faucet (cartridge replacement): $150–$250 including parts
  • Running toilet (flapper, fill valve): $120–$200
  • Toilet replacement: $300–$500 including supply and installation (homeowner supplies toilet)
  • Drain clearing (standard): $150–$300 depending on method (snake vs. hydro jetting)
  • Water heater installation: $400–$700 labour plus the unit cost
  • Shut-off valve replacement: $150–$280
  • Pipe repair / section replacement: $200–$600+ depending on access and length

Emergency Plumbing Rates

After-hours, weekend, and holiday calls carry a premium in Guelph — typically 1.5–2× the standard rate, plus an elevated emergency service call fee of $150–$200+. A burst pipe at 2 AM on a Sunday is genuinely an emergency; a dripping faucet can wait until Monday morning.

Flat Rate vs. Hourly Billing

Some Guelph plumbing companies offer flat-rate pricing for common jobs (drain clearing, faucet replacement). This provides cost certainty upfront. Other companies bill hourly, which can be better value for straightforward jobs that are completed quickly. Ask which method applies to your specific job and get the total in writing before work starts.
